Welcome to the Peninsula Symphonic Winds website!

This site provides you with information about our group. We hope these pages will attract you to join us as a player in our band or as a listener to our programs.

The missions of the Peninsula Symphonic Winds are to provide an opportunity for instrumental musicians to prepare and present the traditional and modern concert band repertoire, to offer stimulating concerts to the public, and to provide a venue for composers of new works for the concert band.

The Peninsula Symphonic Winds (PSW) was formed in the fall of 1996 as a community band affiliated with the Music Department of Los Angeles Harbor College. In September of 2003, the band was organized as an independent, non-profit educational association. The members of the band are volunteers who come from a variety of musical backgrounds, and for whom making music is a labor of love.

Clicking on the buttons in the left margin will take you to pages describing the PSW. Please browse and let us hear from you. Questions about the band may be directed to key personnel who are listed on the CONTACT page.
