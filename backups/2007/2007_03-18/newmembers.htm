New Members Welcome

The Peninsula Symphonic Winds always welcomes new players. We can accommodate almost any band instrument, from bassoon to bells. Some of our members also play in other groups. Some enjoy practicing their skills with their second or third instrument. Skill levels of our players range from moderate to excellent. Music we have played has varied from Richard Rodgers to Stravinski. Players of all band instruments are encouraged to visit us at a rehearsal.

Rehearsals are scheduled from 7:45 to 10:00 p.m. Wednesdays in Room 107, Music Building (MU, north side of campus), Los Angeles Harbor Community College.  Harbor Community College is located at: 1111 Figueroa Place, Wilmington, CA.  The campus is just off the Harbor Freeway, Anaheim Street exit.  Phone: 310-522-8200.   [AREA MAP]  [CAMPUS MAP]

Come experience playing under the excellent direction of our Conductor, Richard Babcock!

Our season begins with the first rehearsal on the first Wednesday in September. Rehearsals continue through the end of June.

We will play three concerts this season. See CONCERTS section.
