October 2006

The Peninsula Symphonic Winds has just begun its eleventh season of providing exciting concert band music to the residents of the Palos Verdes Peninsula and the South Bay. Our opening concert, held in collaboration with the El Camino College Community Band, was a grand success. Under the direction of Richard Babcock the band will perform two more concerts in March and June of 2007, at St. Peter’s By-the-Sea Church and the Norris Pavilion. We hope that you will join us at both of these concerts.

This season also marks the start of our third year as a self-supporting organization. Although Harbor College generously provides rehearsal space in their band room, we no longer receive any financial support from the college. Some of our income is from ticket sales, but that is limited to only one concert per year and is partially offset by the cost of performing at the Norris Pavilion. Admission is free to the rest of our concerts.

Over 80 percent of our financial support comes from the contributions of members and friends such as you. Your generosity has allowed us to buy music, secure concert venues, hire guest artists, and to cover other necessary expenses that have been essential to our growth as an organization and to our growth musically.

As we approach the end of calendar year 2006, this is an excellent time for you to make a contribution if you have not already done so. We have enclosed a form and a return envelope for your use. Remember that contributions to the Peninsula Symphonic Winds are fully deductible for both federal and state income tax purposes, and an acknowledgment letter will be provided to each donor. All contributions are greatly appreciated and will help us to make this a successful concert season.

Adult and family audiences have enjoyed concert band music for over 150 years across America, and we are looking forward to continuing that tradition here in our own community. Thank you in advance for your support.

As a non-profit association, PSW is dependent on its members and friends for support. PSW is a 501(c)(3) organization and contributions are tax deductible to the extent provided by law. Individuals or organizations wishing to contribute to the PSW, may participate at one of several donor levels:
• Sponsor $500 and above
• Patron $100 to $499
• Friend $1 to $99
